# Quickstart

To gain access to the application, users must sign in by providing their credentials. This document outlines the steps required to successfully log in.



<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2026-06-28 223949.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Steps to Sign In

#### 1. Open the Sign-In Page

* Launch the application on your device.
* Navigate to the **Sign In**, which is typically accessible from the main screen.

#### 2. Enter Your Credentials

On the sign-in page, you will see fields for entering your login information:

* **Email**: Input your registered email address in the designated field.
* **Password**: Enter your password in the corresponding field. Ensure that you type it correctly, as passwords are case-sensitive.

#### 3. Complete the Sign-In Process

* After entering your email and password, review the information for accuracy.
* Click on the **"Log In"** button to proceed.

#### 4. Troubleshooting Login Issues

If you encounter any issues while signing in:

* **Forgot Password**: If you cannot remember your password, click on the **"Forgot Password?"** link to initiate a password recovery process.



### Conclusion

By following these steps, users can successfully sign in to the application using your email and password. Ensure that your credentials are entered accurately to avoid login issues. For further assistance or troubleshooting, please reach out to your technical support team.



After successfully logging into the application, users will be directed to the main interface. This document outlines what to expect upon logging in and how to navigate through the application.

The first thing you will see after logging in is the **Dashboard** of the application. The dashboard serves as the central hub for navigating various features and functionalities of the application. Familiarizing yourself with the dashboard layout will enhance your efficiency in using the application. For any questions or further assistance, please consult your user manual or contact technical support.
