# Feedback

## **Feedback Module – New Feedback Submission Form**

***

### **1. Overview**

The Feedback module enables authenticated users to submit structured feedback, complaints, or requests for assistance to the Chamber. The submission interface is presented as a single-page form divided into two logical sections: user identification fields at the top and feedback-specific fields below. This documentation describes the full form layout, field specifications, and interactive elements.

### **2. Form Navigation Bar**

Directly beneath the header, a compact action bar provides form-level controls:

* **Back Arrow:** Returns the user to the previous screen.
* **New Label:** Indicates the current form is creating a new record.
* **Draft Badge:** A subtle tag indicating the form is in draft state.
* **Cancel Button:** A light-styled button to discard the submission and exit.
* **Save Button:** A dark red primary action button with a plus icon, used to persist the feedback record.

***

### **3. Feedback Classification Fields**

* **Feedback Title:** A required text input field (marked with a red asterisk) for entering a concise summary of the feedback.
* **Type Of Feedback / Complaint:** A required dropdown selector with placeholder text "Select The Type...", allowing categorization of the submission.

***

### **4. Target Identification Fields**

* **Organization/Individual:** A required dropdown selector with placeholder text "Select The Organization/individual...", used to identify the party to whom the feedback relates or from whom it originates.

***

### **5. Descriptive Content Fields**

* **Description:** A required large text area with a character limit of 500 words, indicated by the label "(0 / 500 Words)". This field allows the user to provide a detailed narrative of the feedback or complaint.

***

### **6. Impact Assessment Fields**

* **Impact:** A required large text area with a character limit of 250 words, indicated by the label "(0 / 250 Words)". This field captures the perceived impact or consequences of the issue being reported.

***

### **7. Assistance Request Section**

* **Require Chamber's Assistance?:** A required radio button group with two mutually exclusive options: "Yes" and "No". The "Yes" option is currently selected, indicated by a filled red circle.
* **How Do You Need Chambers Assistance:** A required large text area with a character limit of 750 words, indicated by the label "(0 / 750 Words)". This field becomes relevant when the user indicates that Chamber assistance is required.

***

### **8. Document Attachment Section**

* **Documents:** A file upload area labeled "Select Files" with an upload icon, allowing users to attach supporting documentation, images, or evidence relevant to the feedback submission.
