# Management

***

## Management Module — User Interface Documentation

### Overview

This document describes the user interface of the **Management** module within The Ghana Chamber of Mines web portal. The module enables authorized users (managers) to browse, search, and analyze management-level workforce composition data submitted by their respective mining companies. The data displayed in this module originates from the **HR Statistics** section under Data Upload, where management statistics are first captured and submitted.

***

### 1. Management List (View Page)

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2026-07-20 185751.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Management List</p></figcaption></figure>

#### 1.1 Page Header

At the top of the content area, the page title **"Management List"** is displayed in bold, preceded by a back arrow icon (`←`) for navigation to the parent page. To the right of the title, the following controls are available:

* **Search Field**: A text input box with a light gray placeholder text "search", allowing users to filter the list of management records by relevant keywords.
* **Refresh Button**: A circular refresh icon accompanied by the label "Refresh", enabling users to reload the list to reflect the latest data entries.

#### 1.2 Data List

Below the header, a data grid displays management workforce records. The list includes the following column headers, each equipped with sort indicators (up/down arrows) for ascending and descending ordering:

* **Year**: The reporting year for the management workforce data.
* **Month**: The specific month during which the management data was recorded.
* **Company Name**: The name of the mining company associated with the management record.
* **Expatriates Female**: The count of female expatriate employees in management positions during the reporting period.
* **Expatriates Male**: The count of male expatriate employees in management positions during the reporting period.
* **Ghanaians Female**: The count of female Ghanaian employees in management positions during the reporting period.
* **Ghanaians Male**: The count of male Ghanaian employees in management positions during the reporting period.

#### 1.3 Empty State

When no management records exist in the system, the data grid displays an empty state. Centered within the list area is a document inbox icon accompanied by the text **"No data"** in a light gray font. This indicates that no management data has been submitted or recorded for the current view.

***

### 2. Data Source

The information presented in the Management module is sourced from the **HR Statistics** upload section under Data Upload. Management statistics files uploaded through that module are processed and displayed here, providing a consolidated and analytical view of mining company management workforce composition across reporting periods.
