# Human Resources

## Human Resources Modules

The Human Resources area brings together workforce composition and workplace policy records. Authorized users can review data submitted by mining companies through **Data Upload → HR Statistics**.

### Workforce composition

Review workforce data by employment category and seniority level:

* [Direct Workforce](direct-workfore.md) covers employees by department, nationality, and gender.
* [Contractors](contractors.md) covers contracted workers, including skilled and unskilled labour.
* [Management](management.md) and [Board](board.md) cover leadership composition.

### Inclusion and employee support

Review company policies that support equitable and inclusive workplaces:

* [Gender Policies](gender-policies.md) documents gender-related workplace policies.
* [Maternity Policies](maternity-policies.md) records maternity provisions.
* [Disability Policies](disability-policies.md) records disability inclusion policies.
