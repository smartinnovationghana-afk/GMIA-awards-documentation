# Competitions

***

## Competitions Module — User Interface Documentation

### Overview

This document describes the user interface of the **Competitions** module within The Ghana Chamber of Mines web portal. The module enables authorized users (managers) to browse, view, and track safety competitions organized by the Chamber. The module features an interactive list view where clicking on a competition entry navigates to a detailed competition view.

***

### 1. Competitions List (View Page)

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2026-07-20 211148.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>List of Competitions</p></figcaption></figure>

#### 1.1 Page Content

The main content area displays a prominent blue promotional banner representing an upcoming competition. The banner contains the following elements:

* **Competition Title**: The title **"BEst"** is displayed in large white bold text at the top of the banner.
* **Competition Description**: The text **"ho ho ho"** appears below the title in smaller white text, providing a brief description or tagline for the competition.
* **Status Badge**: A light blue badge labeled **"UPCOMING"** indicates that the competition has not yet commenced.
* **Date Range**: A calendar icon accompanied by the text **"08 Jul 2026 - 18 Jul 2026"** displays the scheduled duration of the competition.
* **Trophy Icon**: A faint trophy icon is positioned on the right side of the banner, symbolizing the competitive nature of the event.

#### 1.2 Interaction Behavior

The blue competition banner serves as an interactive element. When a user clicks on the banner, the system navigates to the detailed competition view, displaying comprehensive information about the selected competition including fixtures, participating teams, and match schedules.

### 2. Competition Details (Detail View)

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2026-07-20 211404.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Preview of the Competition</p></figcaption></figure>

#### 2.1 Page Header

The detail view header displays **"Competition"** in bold, preceded by a back arrow (`←`) for returning to the competitions list. On the far right, a **"Close"** button with a circular icon allows the user to exit the detail view.

#### 2.2 Competition Details Card

Below the header, a bordered card titled **"Competition Details"** presents the following information:

* **Status Badge**: A light blue badge labeled **"UPCOMING"** is positioned in the top-right corner of the card, indicating the competition status.
* **Competition Title**: The label "Competition Title" is followed by the value **"BEst"** in bold text.
* **Duration**: The label "Duration" is followed by a calendar icon and the date range **"08 Jul 2026 - 18 Jul 2026"**.
* **Description**: The label "Description" is followed by the text **"ho ho ho"** displayed within a light gray background area.

#### 2.3 Competition Fixtures

Below the details card, a section titled **"Competition Fixtures"** is displayed, accompanied by a badge indicating the number of fixtures (1). This section lists individual matches or events within the competition.

**Fixture Entry**

Each fixture entry contains the following elements:

* **Location Icon**: A trophy icon indicating the venue or location of the fixture.
* **Location Name**:  Identifies the city or venue where the fixture will take place.
* **Status Badge**: A light blue badge labeled **"UPCOMING"** indicates the fixture has not yet occurred.
* **Category Badges**: Two additional badges labeled **"EXE"** and **"EXAMPLE"** provide further classification of the fixture.
* **Fixture Date**: A calendar icon accompanied by the date is displayed on the far right.

**Participating Teams**

Below the fixture header, individual team entries are displayed:

**Team 1: AGA Iduapriem**

* **Team Name**: **"AGA Iduapriem"** is displayed in bold text.
* **Team Category**: A light blue badge labeled **"Aga Birds"** identifies the team classification.
* **Member Count**: A people icon accompanied by the text **"3 Members"** indicates the team size.
* **Member Names**: Three individual member badges display the names **"Falcon"**, **"Sparrow"**, and **"Dove"**.
* **Score**: The label "Score" is followed by the value **"0"** on the far right.

**Team 2: AGA Obuasi**

* **Team Name**: **"AGA Obuasi"** is displayed in bold text.
* **Team Category**: A light blue badge labeled **"Aga Sea Predators"** identifies the team classification.
