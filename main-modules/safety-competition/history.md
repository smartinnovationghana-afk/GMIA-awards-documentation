# History

***

## History Module — User Interface Documentation

### Overview

This document describes the user interface of the **History** module within the Safety Competition section of The Ghana Chamber of Mines web portal. The module enables authorized users (managers) to browse, search, and review the historical record of past safety competitions, including their outcomes and winning participants.

***

### 1. Competition History (View Page)

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2026-07-20 212723.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Competitions History List</p></figcaption></figure>

#### 1.1 Page Header

At the top of the content area, the section title **"Competition History"** is displayed in bold within a light gray header bar. A trophy icon is positioned on the far right of the header bar, symbolizing the competitive and achievement-oriented nature of the module.

#### 1.2 Data List

Below the header, a data grid displays historical competition records. The list includes the following column headers:

* **Title**: The name or title of the competition.
* **Start Date**: The commencement date of the competition.
* **End Date**: The conclusion date of the competition.
* **Winner**: The name of the winning team, company, or individual for the competition.
* **Status**: The final state of the competition, indicating whether it was completed, cancelled, or concluded with a declared winner.

#### 1.3 Empty State

When no historical competition records exist in the system, the data grid displays an empty state. Centered within the list area is a document inbox icon accompanied by the text **"No data"** in a light gray font. This indicates that no past competition data has been recorded or archived for the current view.
