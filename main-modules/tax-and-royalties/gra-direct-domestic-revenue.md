# GRA Direct Domestic Revenue

***

## GRA Direct Domestic Revenue Module — User Interface Documentation

### Overview

This document describes the user interface of the **GRA Direct Domestic Revenue** module within The Ghana Chamber of Mines web portal. The module enables authorized users (managers) to browse, search, and analyze direct domestic revenue data submitted by their respective mining companies. The data displayed in this module originates from the **HR Statistics** section under Data Upload, where revenue statistics are first captured and submitted.

### 1. Direct Domestic Revenue List (View Page)

#### 1.1 Page Header

At the top of the content area, the page title **"Direct Domestic Revenue List"** is displayed in bold, preceded by a back arrow icon (`←`) for navigation to the parent page. To the right of the title, the following controls are available:

* **Search Field**: A text input box with a light gray placeholder text "search", allowing users to filter the list of direct domestic revenue records by relevant keywords.
* **Refresh Button**: A circular refresh icon accompanied by the label "Refresh", enabling users to reload the list to reflect the latest data entries.

#### 1.2 Data List

Below the header, a data grid displays direct domestic revenue records. The list includes the following column headers, each equipped with sort indicators (up/down arrows) for ascending and descending ordering:

* **Company Account**: Identifies the mining company associated with the direct domestic revenue record.
* **Year**: The reporting year for the direct domestic revenue data.
* **Month**: The specific month during which the revenue data was recorded.
* **Mineral Royalty**: The amount of mineral royalty payments made by the mining company to the Ghana Revenue Authority.
* **Social Security**: The amount of social security contributions made by the mining company on behalf of its employees.
* **Employee Income Tax**: The amount of income tax deducted and remitted by the mining company on behalf of its employees.

#### 1.3 Empty State

When no direct domestic revenue records exist in the system, the data grid displays an empty state. Centered within the list area is a document inbox icon accompanied by the text **"No data"** in a light gray font. This indicates that no direct domestic revenue data has been submitted or recorded for the current view.

***

### 2. Data Source

The information presented in the GRA Direct Domestic Revenue module is sourced from the **HR Statistics** upload section under Data Upload. Revenue statistics files uploaded through that module are processed and displayed here, providing a consolidated and analytical view of mining company tax and contribution obligations across reporting periods.
