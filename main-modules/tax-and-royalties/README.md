# Tax & Royalties

## Tax & Royalties

Use these modules to review tax, contribution, royalty, and levy records. Each module shows data submitted by mining companies through **Data Upload → HR Statistics**.

### Available modules

#### [GRA Direct Domestic Revenue](gra-direct-domestic-revenue.md)

Review mineral royalties, social security contributions, and employee income tax. Filter records by company, year, or month.

#### [GRA Corporate Tax](gra-corporate-tax.md)

Review corporate income tax paid to the Ghana Revenue Authority. Records identify the company and reporting period.

#### [Royalties](royalties.md)

Review property rates, ground rent, customs duty, and export development levy. Use the list to compare obligations across reporting periods.

{% hint style="info" %}
Use **Search** to narrow records and **Refresh** to load the latest submitted data.
{% endhint %}
