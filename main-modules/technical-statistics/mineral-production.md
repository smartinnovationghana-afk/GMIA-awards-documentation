# Mineral Production

***

### Overview

This document describes the user interface of the **Minerals Productions** module within The Ghana Chamber of Mines web portal. The module enables authorized users (managers) to browse, search, and analyze mineral production data submitted by their respective mining companies. The data displayed in this module originates from the **Technical Statistics** section under Data Upload, where production statistics are first captured and submitted.

***

### 1. Data Source

The information presented in the Minerals Productions module is sourced from the **Technical Statistics** upload section under Data Upload. Production statistics files uploaded through that module are processed and displayed here, providing a consolidated and analytical view of mining output metrics across reporting periods.

### 2. Minerals Productions List (View Page)

#### 2.1 Page Header

At the top of the content area, the page title **"Minerals Productions List"** is displayed in bold, preceded by a back arrow icon (`←`) for navigation to the parent page. To the right of the title, the following controls are available:

* **Search Field**: A text input box with a light gray placeholder text "search", allowing users to filter the list of mineral production records by relevant keywords.
* **Refresh Button**: A circular refresh icon accompanied by the label "Refresh", enabling users to reload the list to reflect the latest data entries.
* **Filter by Company Dropdown**: A dropdown selection field labeled "Filter by Company" with a downward-facing chevron icon, allowing users to narrow the displayed records to a specific mining company.

#### 2.2 Data List

Below the header, a data grid displays mineral production records. The list includes the following column headers, each equipped with sort indicators (up/down arrows) for ascending and descending ordering:

* **Company Account**: Identifies the mining company associated with the production record.
* **Year**: The reporting year for the mineral production data.
* **Month**: The specific month during which the production data was recorded.
* **Quarter**: The fiscal quarter corresponding to the reporting period.
* **Ore Mined Open Pit**: The quantity of ore extracted through open pit mining operations.
* **Ore Mined Underground**: The quantity of ore extracted through underground mining operations.
* **Ore Processed Plant Production**: The quantity of ore processed at the plant during the reporting period.
* **Average Mill Head Grade**: The average grade of ore fed into the mill for processing.
* **Recovery Rate**: The percentage of valuable minerals successfully recovered during processing.
* **Mineral Production Mine Production**: The total mineral output from the mine during the reporting period.
* **Mineral Exported**: The quantity of minerals exported during the reporting period.



When no mineral production records exist in the system, the data grid displays an empty state. Centered within the list area is a document inbox icon accompanied by the text **"No data"** in a light gray font. This indicates that no mineral production data has been submitted or recorded for the current view.
