# Social Investments

***

### Overview

This document describes the user interface of the **Social Investments** module within The Ghana Chamber of Mines web portal. The module enables authorized users (managers) to browse, search, and analyze social investment expenditure data submitted by their respective mining companies. The data displayed in this module originates from the **Technical Statistics** section under Data Upload, where social investment statistics are first captured and submitted.

***

### 1. Data Source

The information presented in the Social Investments module is sourced from the **Technical Statistics** upload section under Data Upload. Social investment statistics files uploaded through that module are processed and displayed here, providing a consolidated and analytical view of corporate social responsibility expenditure across reporting periods.

### 2. Investments List (View Page)

#### 2.1 Page Header

At the top of the content area, the page title **"Investments List"** is displayed in bold, preceded by a back arrow icon (`←`) for navigation to the parent page. To the right of the title, the following controls are available:

* **Search Field**: A text input box with a light gray placeholder text "search", allowing users to filter the list of social investment records by relevant keywords.
* **Refresh Button**: A circular refresh icon accompanied by the label "Refresh", enabling users to reload the list to reflect the latest data entries.
* **Filter by Company Dropdown**: A dropdown selection field labeled "Filter by Company" with a downward-facing chevron icon, allowing users to narrow the displayed records to a specific mining company.

#### 2.2 Data List

Below the header, a data grid displays social investment records. The list includes the following column headers, each equipped with sort indicators (up/down arrows) for ascending and descending ordering:

* **Company Account**: Identifies the mining company associated with the social investment record.
* **Year**: The reporting year for the social investment data.
* **Month**: The specific month during which the investment data was recorded.
* **Quarter**: The fiscal quarter corresponding to the reporting period.
* **Education ($)**: The monetary amount invested in education initiatives and programs, expressed in United States Dollars.
* **Health ($)**: The monetary amount invested in health initiatives and programs, expressed in United States Dollars.
* **Electricity ($)**: The monetary amount invested in electricity infrastructure and provision, expressed in United States Dollars.
* **Total ($)**: The aggregate monetary value of all social investments for the reporting period, expressed in United States Dollars.



When no social investment records exist in the system, the data grid displays an empty state. Centered within the list area is a document inbox icon accompanied by the text **"No data"** in a light gray font. This indicates that no social investment data has been submitted or recorded for the current view.
