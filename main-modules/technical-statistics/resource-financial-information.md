# Resource Financial Information

***

### Overview

This document describes the user interface of the **Resource Financial Information** module within The Ghana Chamber of Mines web portal. The module enables authorized users (managers) to browse, search, and analyze mineral revenue expenditure data submitted by their respective mining companies. The data displayed in this module originates from the **Technical Statistics** section under Data Upload, where expenditure statistics are first captured and submitted.

***

### 1. Data Source

The information presented in the Resource Financial Information module is sourced from the **Technical Statistics** upload section under Data Upload.  statistics files uploaded through that module are processed and displayed here, providing a consolidated and analytical view of mineral revenue allocation and repatriation metrics across reporting periods.

### 2. Expenditures List (View Page)

#### 2.1 Page Header

At the top of the content area, the page title **"Resource Financial Information List"** is displayed in bold, preceded by a back arrow icon (`←`) for navigation to the parent page. To the right of the title, the following controls are available:

* **Search Field**: A text input box with a light gray placeholder text "search", allowing users to filter the list of expenditure records by relevant keywords.
* **Refresh Button**: A circular refresh icon accompanied by the label "Refresh", enabling users to reload the list to reflect the latest data entries.
* **Filter by Company Dropdown**: A dropdown selection field labeled "Filter by Company" with a downward-facing chevron icon, allowing users to narrow the displayed records to a specific mining company.

#### 2.2 Data List

Below the header, a data grid displays resource financial information records. The list includes the following column headers, each equipped with sort indicators (up/down arrows) for ascending and descending ordering:

* **Company Account**: Identifies the mining company associated with the expenditure record.
* **Year**: The reporting year for the expenditure data.
* **Month**: The specific month during which the expenditure data was recorded.
* **Quarter**: The fiscal quarter corresponding to the reporting period.
* **Mineral Revenue Returned To Country Through BOG**: The amount of mineral revenue returned to the country through the Bank of Ghana, representing official foreign exchange repatriation.
* **Mineral Revenue Returned To Country Through Commercial Banks**: The amount of mineral revenue returned to the country through commercial banking channels, representing alternative repatriation pathways.
* **Amount For Amortizing Loans**: The portion of mineral revenue allocated toward the repayment or amortization of outstanding loans.



When no expenditure records exist in the system, the data grid displays an empty state. Centered within the list area is a document inbox icon accompanied by the text **"No data"** in a light gray font. This indicates that no expenditure data has been submitted or recorded for the current view.
