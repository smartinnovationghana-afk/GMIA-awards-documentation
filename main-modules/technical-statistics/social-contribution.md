# Social Contribution

***

## Social Contributions Module — User Interface Documentation

### Overview

This document describes the user interface of the **Social Contributions** module within The Ghana Chamber of Mines web portal. The module enables authorized users (managers) to browse, search, and analyze social contribution expenditure data submitted by their respective mining companies. The data displayed in this module originates from the **Technical Statistics** section under Data Upload, where social contribution statistics are first captured and submitted.

***

### 1. Social Contributions (View Page)

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2026-07-20 150535.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Social Contributions List</p></figcaption></figure>

#### 1.1 Page Header

At the top of the content area, the page title **"Social Contributions"** is displayed in bold, preceded by a back arrow icon (`←`) for navigation to the parent page. To the right of the title, the following controls are available:

* **Search Field**: A text input box with a light gray placeholder text "search", allowing users to filter the list of social contribution records by relevant keywords.
* **Refresh Button**: A circular refresh icon accompanied by the label "Refresh", enabling users to reload the list to reflect the latest data entries.

#### 1.2 Data List

Below the header, a data grid displays social contribution records. The list includes the following column headers, each equipped with sort indicators (up/down arrows) for ascending and descending ordering:

* **Company Account**: Identifies the mining company associated with the social contribution record.
* **Year**: The reporting year for the social contribution data.
* **Month**: The specific month during which the contribution data was recorded.
* **Quarter**: The fiscal quarter corresponding to the reporting period.
* **Education ($)**: The monetary amount contributed toward education initiatives and programs, expressed in United States Dollars.
* **Health ($)**: The monetary amount contributed toward health initiatives and programs, expressed in United States Dollars.
* **Electricity ($)**: The monetary amount contributed toward electricity infrastructure and provision, expressed in United States Dollars.
* **Total ($)**: The aggregate monetary value of all social contributions for the reporting period, expressed in United States Dollars.

#### 1.3 Empty State

When no social contribution records exist in the system, the data grid displays an empty state. Centered within the list area is a document inbox icon accompanied by the text **"No data"** in a light gray font. This indicates that no social contribution data has been submitted or recorded for the current view.

***

### 2. Data Source

The information presented in the Social Contributions module is sourced from the **Technical Statistics** upload section under Data Upload. Social contribution statistics files uploaded through that module are processed and displayed here, providing a consolidated and analytical view of corporate social responsibility expenditure across reporting periods.
