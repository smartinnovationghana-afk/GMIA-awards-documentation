# Awards Submission

## Ghana Mining Industry Awards (GMIA) – Member Nominations Portal Documentation

***

### A. MY NOMINATIONS DASHBOARD

***

#### 1. Module Overview

The **My Nominations** module serves as the dedicated self-service portal for members and nominators participating in the Ghana Mining Industry Awards program. This interface enables authorized users to initiate new award nominations, monitor the status of existing submissions, track form completion progress, and manage their complete nomination portfolio within a single, intuitive dashboard. The module is designed to streamline the submission experience while providing real-time visibility into nomination deadlines and submission status.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2026-07-20 221241.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>My Nominations List</p></figcaption></figure>

***

#### 2. Module Header

**2.1 Section Title**

* **Iconography:** Trophy/award icon (gold) indicating nomination context
* **Label:** "My Nominations"
* **Contextual Description:** "Manage and track your award submissions"
* **Purpose:** Establishes the module's function as the member's personal nomination management center

***

#### 3. Nomination Deadline Countdown

This element provides continuous temporal awareness to ensure members remain informed of submission windows.

**3.1 Countdown Timer**

* **Visual Design:** Green-bordered notification card with clock icon
* **Label:** "NOMINATIONS CLOSE IN"
* **Display Format:** Days, Hours, Minutes, and Seconds (e.g., "357 D : 12 H : 42 M : 43 S")
* **Function:** Presents a real-time, decrementing countdown to the official nomination deadline
* **Purpose:** Creates urgency awareness and enables members to plan submission timelines effectively; ensures no nominations are missed due to deadline oversight

***

#### 4. Primary Action Control

**4.1 Start New Nomination Button**

* **Visual Design:** Primary action button with solid blue background and dropdown arrow
* **Label:** "Start New Nomination"
* **Iconography:** Downward chevron/arrow indicating dropdown functionality
* **Function:** Initiates the nomination creation workflow; the dropdown arrow suggests the presence of category selection options upon interaction
* **Purpose:** Serves as the primary entry point for members to begin the award submission process; enables selection from available award categories

***

#### 5. Nomination Records Display

The module presents a structured overview of all nominations associated with the member's account.

**5.1 Data Display Fields**

The nomination list presents the following structured data fields for each submission:

* **Award Category** — The specific award classification under which the nomination was submitted
* **Nominee** — The name of the individual, organization, or entity being nominated for the award
* **Form Completion** — Visual progress indicator showing the percentage of required fields completed within the nomination form
* **Status** — Current lifecycle state of the nomination (e.g., Draft, Submitted, Under Review, Approved, Declined)
* **Last Updated** — Timestamp indicating the most recent modification or autosave event for the nomination
* **Action** — Operational controls enabling members to edit, review, submit, or delete individual nominations

**5.2 Empty State Indicator**

* **Visual Design:** Centralized folder/box icon with "No data" label
* **Context:** Indicates the member has not yet initiated any nominations within the current awards cycle
* **Purpose:** Provides clear feedback and prompts the member to begin their first nomination through the Start New Nomination action

***

### &#x20;AWARD CATEGORY SELECTION DROPDOWN

***

#### 1. Overview

The **Award Category Selection Dropdown** is the interactive overlay that appears when a member clicks the **Start New Nomination** button. This interface presents the complete portfolio of available award categories, enabling members to select the appropriate classification for their nomination. The dropdown serves as the critical bridge between the member's intent to nominate and the structured form-building process.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2026-07-20 221331.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>List of the nomination</p></figcaption></figure>

***

#### 2. Dropdown Trigger

**2.1 Activation Mechanism**

* **Trigger:** Click action on the "Start New Nomination" button
* **Visual Feedback:** Dropdown panel slides or fades into view below the button
* **Purpose:** Reveals category options without navigating away from the My Nominations dashboard

***

#### 3. Category List Structure

The dropdown presents all available award categories as a vertically scrollable list, with each category displayed as a selectable row.

**3.1 Category Row Design**

Each category entry within the dropdown follows a consistent presentation format:

* **Iconography:** Trophy/award icon (gold) positioned left of the category name, reinforcing the awards context
* **Category Name:** Full award classification title in uppercase text
* **Selection Interaction:** Click-to-select action that initiates the nomination form for the chosen category

***

#### 4. Available Award Categories

The dropdown presents the following award classifications for member selection:

* **BEST MINING FIRM IN DIVERSITY AND INCLUSION** — Recognizes organizations demonstrating exemplary commitment to diversity and inclusion policies, practices, and outcomes
* **BEST PERFORMER IN CORPORATE SOCIAL INVESTMENT (CSI)** — Honors entities with outstanding corporate social responsibility and community investment initiatives
* **BEST PERFORMER IN ENGINEERING, PROCUREMENT, AND CONSTRUCTION MANAGEMENT (EPCM)** — Acknowledges excellence in engineering, procurement, and construction management delivery
* **BEST PERFORMER IN INNOVATION** — Celebrates organizations driving technological and operational innovation within the mining sector
* **BEST PERFORMER IN LOCAL MANUFACTURING OF MINING INPUTS** — Recognizes contributions to local content and domestic manufacturing capacity
* **BEST PERFORMER IN MINE SUPPLIES & SERVICES** — Honors excellence in the provision of mining supplies and support services
* **FEMALE MINING PROFESSIONAL OF THE YEAR - TECHNICAL** — Recognizes outstanding technical contributions by women in mining
* **FEMALE MINING PROFESSIONAL OF THE YEAR - NON-TECHNICAL** — Honors exceptional non-technical leadership and contributions by women in mining
* **FINANCIAL DEAL OF THE YEAR** — Acknowledges significant and impactful financial transactions within the mining industry
* **LIFETIME ACHIEVEMENT AWARD** — Celebrates enduring contributions and legacy impact on the Ghanaian mining sector
* **MINING PERSONALITY OF THE YEAR (THE PRESIDENT'S AWARD)** — The highest individual honor, recognizing exceptional leadership and influence in mining

***

#### 5. Member Selection Workflow

The typical category selection workflow follows this sequence:

1. **Activate** — Click the Start New Nomination button to reveal the category dropdown
2. **Review** — Examine the complete list of available award categories
3. **Select** — Click the desired category to initiate the nomination form
4. **Proceed** — The system loads the category-specific nomination form with pre-configured fields and evaluation criteria



## B. Filling A Nomination Form

***

**1. Module Overview**

The **Nomination Form** module serves as the comprehensive submission interface for members participating in the Ghana Mining Industry Awards program. This interface enables authorized nominators to complete structured award applications with detailed narrative responses, quantitative data, and supporting documentation. The form is dynamically generated based on the selected award category, ensuring that all submissions address the specific evaluation criteria and information requirements defined by the awards administration.

#### 2. Navigation & Action Controls

**2.1 Back to Dashboard Button**

* **Visual Design:** Secondary outlined button with left arrow icon
* **Iconography:** Left-pointing arrow
* **Label:** "Back to Dashboard"
* **Function:** Returns the member to the My Nominations dashboard without submitting the current form
* **Purpose:** Provides safe exit from the nomination workflow while preserving any saved draft progress

**2.2 Save Draft Button**

* **Visual Design:** Secondary outlined button with save/document icon
* **Iconography:** Save icon
* **Label:** "Save Draft"
* **Function:** Persists all entered form data to the system without finalizing the submission

**2.3 Submit Final Button**

* **Visual Design:** Primary action button with solid blue background and checkmark icon
* **Iconography:** Checkmark/confirmation icon
* **Label:** "Submit Final"
* **Function:** Finalizes the nomination, validates all required fields, and formally transmits the submission to the awards administration
* **Validation:** Enforces completion of all mandatory fields before activation; typically accompanied by confirmation dialog to prevent accidental submission

***

#### 3. Category Header & Progress Tracking

**3.1 Award Category Title**

* **Display Format:** Uppercase text with red left border accent
* **Current Display:** "BEST MINING FIRM IN DIVERSITY AND INCLUSION"
* **Purpose:** Provides continuous visual confirmation of the selected award category throughout the form completion process

**3.2 Form Completion Indicator**

* **Visual Design:** Horizontal progress bar with percentage display and green badge
* **Current Display:** "0%" with "0% Form Completion" badge
* **Function:** Dynamically updates to reflect the proportion of required fields completed
* **Purpose:** Provides real-time feedback on submission readiness and motivates members toward completion

**3.3 Print Preview Button**

* **Visual Design:** Secondary outlined button with printer icon
* **Iconography:** Printer icon
* **Label:** "Print Preview"
* **Function:** Generates a print-optimized view of the current form state for offline review or physical documentation
* **Purpose:** Enables members to review their submission in a consolidated format before final submission

#### 6. Detailed Submission Sections

This section contains the substantive evaluation criteria requiring detailed narrative responses from the nominator.

**6.1 Section Header**

* **Label:** "Detailed Submission Sections"

**6.2 Criterion 1: D\&I Policy and Strategic Objectives**

* **Label:** "Describe your organisation's diversity and inclusion (D\&I) policy and strategic objectives. Include scope of the policy (gender, physically challenged, inclusion, etc.); specific targets, timelines, and key performance indicators (KPIs) and governance structures and accountability mechanisms." (marked with red asterisk indicating required field)
* **Type:** Multi-line text area with resizable corner
* **Function:** Captures the foundational governance framework for diversity and inclusion within the nominated organization
* **Purpose:** Assesses the existence, comprehensiveness, and strategic alignment of D\&I policies

**6.3 Criterion 2: D\&I Initiatives**

* **Label:** "Outline the initiatives implemented in 2025 to promote diversity and inclusion. Include recruitment and retention strategies; training, awareness, and sensitisation programmes; workplace accessibility improvements (including for physically challenged persons) and efforts to promote diversity in leadership and decision-making" (marked with red asterisk indicating required field)
* **Type:** Multi-line text area with resizable corner
* **Function:** Documents active programs and interventions undertaken during the award year
* **Purpose:** Evaluates operational execution and tangible commitment to D\&I objectives

**6.4 Criterion 3: Evidence of Outcomes**

* **Label:** "Provide evidence of the outcomes achieved from your D\&I initiatives in 2025. Include changes in workforce diversity (e.g., % increase in female employees or leadership); representation of physically challenged persons; employee retention, promotion, or engagement outcomes and any relevant survey results or performance indicators." (marked with red asterisk indicating required field)
* **Type:** Multi-line text area with resizable corner
* **Function:** Captures quantitative and qualitative evidence of D\&I program impact
* **Purpose:** Measures tangible results and validates the effectiveness of stated initiatives

**6.5 Criterion 4: Sustainability of D\&I Initiatives**

* **Label:** "Describe how your organisation ensures the sustainability of D\&I initiatives. Include budgetary spend; institutional structures (e.g., committees, policies, reporting lines) and monitoring, evaluation, and reporting mechanisms." (marked with red asterisk indicating required field)

* **Function:** Documents long-term commitment mechanisms and resource allocation for D\&I programs
* **Purpose:** Assesses institutional embedding and future viability of diversity and inclusion efforts

**7. Workforce Demographics Data Section**

This section collects structured quantitative data to support the narrative responses.

**7.1 Section Instruction**

* **Physically Challenged Persons** — Quantitative headcount of physically challenged employees across gender categories

* **Male Row** — Captures workforce data for male employees
